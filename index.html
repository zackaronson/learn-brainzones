Marcey Aronson & Debbie Leonard have been helping students and teachers work better together for over 30 years.

Our names are Marcey Aronson and Debbie Leonard, two certified master teachers uniquely positioned as experts in the field of emotions in education. Prior to entering our classroom, every student we ever worked with, over twenty-five years, carried the stigma of failing. Lacking academic success, emotional awareness, motivation, self-esteem and/or emotional regulation. As with many inner-city kids, many lived with exposure to repetitive Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACE’S). Research reveals, “emotions drive people and people drive performance,” (6 Seconds) and emotions were at the core of our students’ failures.

Learn how to use emotions more effectively through our customizable neuro-inspired emotional Brainzone (BZ) Models. These models guided both our instruction and our interactions in the classroom, leading us to better understand both ourselves and our students’ motivations, behaviors, and educational needs. Ultimately turning their failures into both school and lifelong successes; backed by both quantitative and qualitative data. These simple, yet comprehensive models offer crucial insights to elucidate the impact of emotions on teaching, learning, and behavior. When teachers are able to understand and recognize emotional zones, through the BZ models, they are building their emotional intelligence and positioning themselves to succeed.

With Brainzones knowledge comes insight, to yourself and your students, leaving you in a position to both predict and/or shift outcomes. Emotional leveraging is the most powerful device educators have at their disposal, and Brainzones will offer educators a comprehensive how-to guide, with tools, for strategically applying them to better their lives, their classrooms, and the lives of their students.
